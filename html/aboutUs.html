About Bear Essentials Academy
Welcome to Bear Essentials Academy Daycare

In, 2008, we opened our first center on 5400 S. Taylorsville, UT. Prior to opening this location, the owner Wendy Pendleton provided childcare in her home for 13 years. Her goal for providing childcare was always to offer quality childcare at affordable rates and that continues to be our focus. In 2018, we opened our second center in Taylorsville located on redwood road. This has allowed us to continue to offer quality care to more and more families.

Our daycare centers are smaller than most. This allows us to have smaller class sizes and provides us with the opportunity to really get to know each child and their family. Both centers offer an enriched curriculum in a home like environment.

Wendy is committed to providing the best care possible by employing the best staff possible. Many of our teachers have been with us since we opened and are spread between both centers. All of our staff take courses each year to continue their education in the field of child development. Certifications held by staff members include: CDA (Child Development Associate) and NAC (National Administrator Credential). All teachers are certified in First Aid and CPR and re-certify on a regular basis.

Our Story
We believe in the kids

We want to give your child the best care possible, at a reasonable rate. We will strive to make each child feel loved and valued. Food, Health and Nutrition
The kids love our food!

At Bear Essentials Academy we only serve healthy nutritious meals. While your child is here they will enjoy breakfast, A.M snack, Lunch, and P.M snack. We participate in the National Food Program and follow all the healthy food guidelines. We serve whole grain items at least once a day, fruit at least twice a day, vegetables at least once a day, and milk 2-3 times a day. Children also have access to water all day long and classes take water breaks to encourage water drinking.
Both locations are Top Star endorsed. This means we go beyond the requirements of the National Food Program. Some of these additional nutritional choices include only offering juice 2 times a week and offering whole grains more than once a day. Top Star endorsement also means our staff has been trained and certified in healthy habits. Children are taken outside twice a day to enjoy the benefits of outdoor play and exercise. In class activities such as dance and music give children the opportunity to move and have fun with exercise as well.
